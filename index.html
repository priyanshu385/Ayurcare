Diseases

Explore exercises and yoga recommendations for various health conditions.

Diabetes

A chronic condition that affects blood sugar regulation.

Yoga Poses: Vajrasana, Dhanurasana, Pavanamuktasana
Exercises: Brisk walking, cycling

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

A hormonal imbalance affecting women of reproductive age.

Yoga Poses: Dhanurasana, Sarvangasana, Surya Namaskar
Exercises: Aerobics, light jogging

Back Pain

A common ailment affecting the lower or upper back.

Yoga Poses: Bhujangasana, Balasana, Tadasana
Exercises: Cat-cow stretches, swimming

Hypertension

Also known as high blood pressure, a condition that affects the arteries.

Yoga Poses: Shavasana, Viparita Karani, Balasana
Exercises: Deep breathing, walking

Yoga & Exercise Library

Discover yoga poses and exercises to improve your health.

Dhanurasana

Involves lying on the stomach and holding the ankles, creating a bow shape.Strengthens the back and abdominal muscles, improves digestion.


Diseases : Beneficial for diabetes, PCOS, and back pain.

Vajrasana

A seated pose with legs folded under the body, keeping the spine straight.Aids digestion, calms the mind, and enhances focus.


Diseases : Ideal for diabetes, digestive issues, and insomnia.

Bhujangasana

Lying on the stomach and lifting the upper body with the help of arms.Strengthens the spine, improves lung capacity, and reduces stress.


Diseases : Helps with asthma, back pain, and heart diseases.

Shavasana

Involves lying flat on the back in a relaxed position.Promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and improves sleep.


Diseases : Useful for hypertension, depression, and insomnia.
